About Me

I'm Abhishek Kumar, a Technical Officer at a leading Government R&D University in India. With over 8 years of experience in electronics hardware development, I specialize in designing, testing, and prototyping embedded systems and custom electronics solutions.

From academic labs to tech startups and industrial R&D, I’ve worked across diverse environments—transforming raw ideas into robust, functional prototypes.
